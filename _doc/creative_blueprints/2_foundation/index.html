Foundation Validated

Word puzzle games feel repetitive, isolated, or too academic—players lack engagement hooks, progression milestones, and reasons to return daily.

The Problem

Word puzzle games feel isolated and repetitive. Players need engaging progression, daily reward loops, and meaningful milestones that make them want to return.

The Solution

Casual gamers and families want engaging word games that reward discovery, offer meaningful progression, and provide daily reasons to play. Traditional word puzzles lack story, community, and tangible reward loops that keep players invested long-term.

Target Audience

Families and casual gamers aged 8+, word-game enthusiasts, competitive players seeking leaderboards

Personal Motivation

Create a premium-feel word game that is more engaging than Wordle, more accessible than Scrabble, and more educational than typical mobile puzzles.
